Margherita recipe

Decription:

Embark on a culinary journey with the timeless delight of pizza, an iconic dish transcending borders. In this rendition, savor the simplicity of a classic Margherita Pizza- a perfect harmony of fresh ingredients and authentic flavors, reminiscent of the sun-soaked landscapes of Italy

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cups fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1 large tomato, thinly sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish
  • Extra-virgin olive oil, for drizzling
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cornmeal or flour for dusting

Steps you need to follow:

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat.
  2. Dust your work surface with flour or cornmeal.
  3. Roll out the pizza dough to your desired thickness.
  4. Place the rolled-out dough on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  5. Arrange the sliced mozzarella and tomato evenly on top of the sauce.
  6. Season the pizza with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Carefully transfer the pizza to the preheated oven or onto the pizza stone. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
