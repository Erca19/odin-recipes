Cacio e pepe recipe

Decription:

Embark on a culinary journey with the timeless simplicity of Cacio e Pepe, an Italian classic celebrated for its elegance and rich, peppery allure. Originating from the heart of Rome, this pasta dish captures the essence of minimalist perfection, where the beauty lies in the marriage of a few quality ingredients. As we delve into the steps to create Cacio e Pepe, savor the anticipation of a dish that epitomizes the art of pasta simplicity.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 cup Pecorino Romano cheese, finely grated
  • 1 tablespoon black peppercorns, freshly ground
  • Extra-virgin olive oil (optional)
  • Salt for pasta water

Steps you need to follow:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti until al dente. Reserve about a cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. In a pan over medium heat, toast the freshly ground black peppercorns until fragrant.
  3. While the pasta is still hot, toss it into the bowl with the cheese mixture, ensuring the strands are well coated.
  4. Serve immediately, allowing the simplicity of Cacio e Pepe to shine as a testament to the sublime marriage of cheese and pepper.
